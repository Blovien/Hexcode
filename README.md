# Hexcode

Hexcode is a component-based spellcasting system. 

It is a two-phase system. Crafting mode and Casting mode

## **Crafting Mode**
Crafting mode is where you setup your spells at your base. It is a space for you to draw glyphs, combine them, and add them to your book
### Pedestals
Pedestals hold your book and apply some modifier to it
### Obelisks
Obelisks modify your crafting area in some way (larger, faster, more accurate, etc)

## **Casting Mode**
Casting mode is the mode you enter when in combat or out on the go. It lets you combine the glyphs you made in Crafting Mode to make scenario-specific responses to thing. 
### Hexbook
The book you hold that keeps your glyphs in it. Limited number of glyphs / book
### Hexstaff
The item you use to combine and cast your spells. Determines where around you they spawn as well as modifies the recastability of your magic
